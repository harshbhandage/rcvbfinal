About Us

Rotaract Club of Venugram Belgaum sponsored by Rotary Club of Venugram Belgaum is proud and honored to be the Home Club Of DRR Rtr. Aniket Jadhav for the RIY 22-23.
RCVB is one of the leading and most active club in the entire 3170 District. Chartered on 10th September 2012, it is the most dedicated club in the whole RID 3170 with many special recognition and awards like Best Club for the RIY 2019-20 , Best Emerging Club RIY 2018-19, Best Club of Belgaum Zone for RIY 2019-20 among others.
